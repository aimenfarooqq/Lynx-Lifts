About Lynx Lifts

Lynx Lifts is community-based travel coordination platform designed for college students who don’t have cars. The event introduces students to a safer, easier and more efficient way of planning off-campus trips by connecting them with peers who share similar travel needs. Whether it’s running errands, attending appointments or going shopping, the platform helps students form carpools and build connections. The intended audience is primarily freshmen and students without personal transportation, though all students are welcome to participate.

Why Lynx Lifts?

  • Cheaper
  • Safer
  • Meet other Students
